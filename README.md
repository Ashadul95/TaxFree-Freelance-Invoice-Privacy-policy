# Privacy Policy for TaxFree: Freelance Invoice

**Last Updated:** January 2, 2026

## Introduction

TaxFree: Freelance Invoice ("we", "our", or "the app") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we handle your information when you use our mobile application.

## Information We Collect

### Personal Information You Provide

When using TaxFree: Freelance Invoice, you may enter the following information:

- **Business Information**: Business name, email, phone number, and address
- **Client Information**: Client names, email addresses, phone numbers, and addresses
- **Invoice Data**: Invoice numbers, items, amounts, dates, and descriptions
- **Expense Records**: Expense categories, amounts, dates, and descriptions
- **App Settings**: Currency preferences, tax rates, and theme preferences

### Information Automatically Collected

- **Device Information**: We may collect device type, operating system version, and app version for compatibility purposes
- **Ad-Related Data**: Google Mobile Ads may collect advertising identifiers and usage data for ad delivery (see Third-Party Services section)

## How We Use Your Information

We use the information you provide solely for the following purposes:

- **Invoice Generation**: Create and format professional invoices with your business and client information
- **Expense Tracking**: Record and categorize your business expenses
- **Client Management**: Store and organize client contact information
- **PDF Creation**: Generate PDF documents of your invoices for sharing
- **Daily Notifications**: Send daily tax tips if you enable this feature
- **App Functionality**: Maintain your preferences and settings

## Data Storage and Security

### Local Storage Only

**Important**: All your data is stored locally on your device using secure local storage (AsyncStorage). We do NOT:

- Upload your data to any cloud servers
- Create user accounts or profiles
- Sync your data across devices
- Access your data remotely
- Share your data with any third parties (except as noted below)

### Security Measures

- All data remains on your device and is protected by your device's security measures
- No data is transmitted over the internet except when you share invoices via email/messaging apps
- We implement industry-standard security practices in our code

### Data Control

You have complete control over your data:

- All data can be deleted by uninstalling the app
- No data recovery is possible after uninstallation
- You manually control when and how to share invoice PDFs

## Third-Party Services

### Google Mobile Ads

Our app displays advertisements provided by Google Mobile Ads (AdMob). Google may collect:

- Advertising ID
- Device information
- App usage data
- Location data (general, not precise)

Google uses this information to:

- Deliver personalized advertisements
- Measure ad performance
- Prevent fraud and abuse

For more information about Google's data practices, please visit:

- [Google Privacy Policy](https://policies.google.com/privacy)
- [Google AdMob Partners](https://support.google.com/admob/answer/9012903)

You can opt out of personalized advertising by adjusting your device settings:

- **Android**: Settings → Google → Ads → Opt out of Ads Personalization
- **iOS**: Settings → Privacy → Advertising → Limit Ad Tracking

### Expo Platform

Our app is built using Expo, which may collect anonymous usage statistics to improve the platform. Expo does not have access to your personal data stored in the app.

## Sharing Your Information

We do not share, sell, or rent your personal information to third parties. Your data remains on your device.

**When You Share Invoices**: When you generate and share invoice PDFs via email, WhatsApp, or other messaging apps, you are directly sharing those documents through your chosen platform. We do not control how those platforms handle your data.

## Permissions

Our app requests the following permissions:

- **Notifications** (POST_NOTIFICATIONS): To send you daily tax tips if you enable this feature
- **Storage**: To save invoice PDFs on your device
- **Internet**: To display advertisements

These permissions are used solely for the stated purposes and do not grant us access to other data on your device.

## Children's Privacy

TaxFree: Freelance Invoice is intended for use by freelancers and business owners who are 18 years of age or older. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you believe we have inadvertently collected information from a child, please contact us immediately.

## Data Retention

Since all data is stored locally on your device:

- Data persists until you delete it manually or uninstall the app
- We have no access to your data and cannot delete it remotely
- Uninstalling the app permanently deletes all your data

## Your Rights

You have the right to:

- Access all your data within the app
- Modify or delete any information you've entered
- Export your invoice PDFs
- Disable notifications at any time
- Uninstall the app and delete all data

## Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by:

- Updating the "Last Updated" date at the top of this policy
- Posting the new Privacy Policy in the app or on our website
- Providing an in-app notification for significant changes

Continued use of the app after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.

## International Data Transfers

Since all data is stored locally on your device, there are no international data transfers. However, Google Ads may process data in accordance with their privacy policy, which may involve international transfers.
